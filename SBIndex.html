Welcome to Skyburger Company

Are you tired of ranks? Fed up with mandatory attendance? Would you like to do more than the same job every time? Welcome to Skyburger.

We are a semi-casual Arma 3 unit, blending fun and organization. While we encourage a certain level of decorum, we also welcome shenanigans—just make sure they don't disrupt others' experiences.

Anyone can sign up for any role, mission by mission. All we ask is that you know how to perform the role's duties. You will be placed on probation for your first two missions, during which you'll observe how we operate. During probation, you cannot take on leadership positions and are expected to learn names and voices while gauging our playstyle. Once probation ends, you'll gain full access to roles and channels.

Remember, we're here to have fun. If you think twice about doing something "funny," it's probably best not to do it. We're all about creating an enjoyable and respectful environment for everyone.

What We Offer

  • Flexible Operations: Our zeuses set operation times, usually starting around 7 PM CST, with the number of ops varying week to week.
  • Diverse Gameplay: No restrictions on time periods or factions—one week you might be fighting as NATO, and the next, a rebel militia.
  • Custom Modpack: A robust mod collection, including RHS, totaling around 80GB to deliver immersive experiences.
  • Dedicated Server & TFAR: We provide a reliable server and use TFAR for immersive communication.
  • Inclusive Community: LGBTQ+ friendly and welcoming to players of all experience levels.

Who We Are

Founded in August 2021, Skyburger Company is a welcoming community that’s LGBTQ+ friendly and open to players of all experience levels. Whether you’re a battle-hardened Arma veteran or just stepping into the field, you’ll find a home here.
